Five Facts about Taylor Hawkins

Did You Know?...

  1. Taylor Hawkins joined the legendary Foo Fighters back in 1997.
  2. Oliver Taylor Hawkins was born on 17 February 1972 in Forth Worth, Texas, and began playing the drums at the tender age of ten.
  3. Taylor Hawkins' great idols: Stewart Copeland from The Police and Phil Collins.
  4. After being a member of a few cover bands, he finally became part of Alanis Morissette's backing band.
  5. There he was discovered by Dave Grohl, who was just looking for a new drummer for the Foo Fighters
